Instructions

  • The rules are simple: you vs a friend in the ultimate player vs player tic tac toe battle. First to 3 in a row wins!

  • Controls: Take turns with Player 1 [X] starting first, and Player 2 [O] going second. The game will automatically detect when one player has won, or if there is a tie!

  • Press the play again button to restart the game, and have fun!
